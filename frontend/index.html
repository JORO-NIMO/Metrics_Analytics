About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Maternal Health Uganda emerged from a simple yet powerful observation: too many Ugandan mothers were dying from preventable complications during pregnancy and childbirth. Our founder, Dr. Sarah Namutebi, a practicing obstetrician in Kampala, witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of limited access to maternal healthcare information.

What started as a small community outreach program in five villages has grown into a comprehensive digital platform reaching over 50,000 mothers across Uganda. Today, we combine technology with traditional healthcare delivery to ensure that every mother receives the care and information she deserves.

Why Choose Us

Built for Ugandan Mothers

Maternal Health Uganda integrates technology with grassroots community engagement, creating sustainable impact that reaches the most vulnerable populations. Our partnerships with the Ugandan Ministry of Health, WHO, and UNICEF validate our approach and ensure alignment with national health priorities.
